## About Credits

Credits are the currency within ContactShip that allow you to make calls and use our AI voice agents. Each call or interaction consumes credits based on its duration and type.

## Credit Usage

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Outbound Calls">
    Credits are consumed based on:
    - Call duration
    - AI agent interaction time
    - Call outcome (successful connection, voicemail, etc.)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Inbound Calls">
    Credit consumption varies by:
    - Length of conversation
    - AI processing requirements
    - Additional features used
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Web Widget Calls">
    Credits are used for:
    - Browser-based calls
    - AI agent engagement
    - Call duration
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Monitoring Usage

Keep track of your credit consumption through:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Dashboard Overview">
    View your current balance and recent usage directly on your main dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Detailed Reports">
    Access comprehensive reports showing credit usage by:
    - Campaign
    - Channel
    - Time period
    - Agent type
  </Step>
  <Step title="Usage Alerts">
    Set up notifications for:
    - Low credit balance
    - High usage patterns
    - Automatic recharge events
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
**Credit Management Tips**
- Monitor usage patterns to predict credit needs
- Set up alerts before credits run low
- Review detailed reports to optimize usage
- Consider automatic recharge for uninterrupted service
</Tip>
