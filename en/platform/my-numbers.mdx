## Verify Your Number

### Verification Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access My Numbers">
    Navigate to the **Organizations** menu and select **My Numbers**. You'll find an add new number button at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Number">
    Enter the phone number you want to verify and click **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Call Verification">
    You'll receive an automated call to your number. On screen, you'll see a code that you'll need to enter using your phone's keypad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Once you've entered the code correctly, you'll see a green checkmark on the platform confirming successful verification.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
This process verifies your ownership of the number and allows your contacts to see this number when you make calls. It doesn't affect your phone plan or incur additional charges.
</Note>

### Assign an AI agent to your number

Once purchased, the number will appear in **My Numbers** with an **offline** status (because it doesn't have an agent assigned yet). To activate it:

1. Click **Manage Agent** on your number.
2. Select any of the AI agents you've already created.
3. Click **Assign Agent**.
