## What is ContactShip?

ContactShip is a powerful AI voice communication platform that helps businesses automate and scale their phone interactions while maintaining a natural, human-like experience. Our platform combines advanced AI technology with easy-to-use tools to handle various communication needs.

## Communication Channels

### 1. Outbound Campaigns
Launch automated call campaigns for:
- Lead generation and qualification
- Sales and follow-ups
- Appointment scheduling
- Payment reminders
- Customer surveys

### 2. Inbound Call Handling
Set up AI agents to:
- Answer customer inquiries
- Provide 24/7 support
- Schedule appointments
- Route calls to appropriate departments
- Handle common customer service tasks

### 3. Web Widget Integration
Embed our call widget in your website to:
- Enable instant voice connections
- Provide immediate customer support
- Convert web visitors into leads
- Schedule callbacks automatically

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up and access your ContactShip dashboard to begin setting up your communication system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Numbers">
    Configure your phone numbers - verify existing ones or purchase new ones through our platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create AI Agents">
    Design your AI voice agents with specific personalities, skills, and conversation flows tailored to your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Channel">
    Decide how you want to deploy your agents - through campaigns, inbound calls, or web widgets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch & Monitor">
    Start your communication automation and track results through our comprehensive analytics dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
