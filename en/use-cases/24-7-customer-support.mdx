## The problem

Your customers call outside business hours and no one answers. Or your support team can't keep up with the volume of incoming calls. Hiring more people is expensive and service quality varies.

## The solution

With ContactShip you can **buy a phone number** and assign it an AI agent that handles every incoming call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The agent answers questions, resolves issues, schedules appointments, and escalates to humans when needed.
