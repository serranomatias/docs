## Explore what you can do with ContactShip

Each use case includes a step-by-step tutorial so you can implement it yourself in minutes.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Qualify leads automatically" icon="phone-arrow-up-right" href="/en/use-cases/lead-qualification">
    When a lead fills out a form, they get an instant call from an AI agent that qualifies them, schedules meetings, and collects key information.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI-powered collections" icon="file-invoice-dollar" href="/en/use-cases/automated-collections">
    Launch collection campaigns where your agent negotiates payment commitments and notifies you automatically when it succeeds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="24/7 customer support" icon="headset" href="/en/use-cases/24-7-customer-support">
    Buy a phone number and assign it an AI agent that handles incoming calls 24 hours a day with no human intervention.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mass sales campaigns" icon="bullhorn" href="/en/use-cases/sales-campaigns">
    Load thousands of contacts and launch a campaign where your agent sells, schedules, and personalizes each conversation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
