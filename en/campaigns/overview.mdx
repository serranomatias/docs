### Outbound Call Campaigns

To set up outbound call campaigns, follow these steps:

1. **Configure Campaigns**: Define the parameters of your campaign, such as the call script, schedule, and objectives.
2. **Assign an Agent**: Select the AI voice agent that will handle the calls for this campaign.
3. **Upload Contacts**: Provide a list of contacts that the agent will call. Ensure the contact information is accurate and up-to-date.
