## What are Call Campaigns?

Call campaigns allow you to automate outbound calls at scale using our AI voice agents. Whether you need to reach hundreds or thousands of contacts, our platform ensures each conversation is natural and effective.

## Setting Up a Campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Campaign">
    Navigate to the Campaigns section and click "New Campaign". Define your campaign name and objectives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select or Create Agent">
    Choose an existing AI agent or create a new one specifically for this campaign. Configure the agent's personality and conversation flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Contacts">
    Import your contact list in CSV format or connect your CRM to automatically sync contacts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set up campaign parameters:
    - Call schedule and time zones
    - Retry attempts for unanswered calls
    - Call concurrency
    - Campaign duration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and Launch">
    Run test calls to verify the agent's performance, then launch your campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
**Campaign Optimization Tips**
- Start with a small test group to refine your approach
- Schedule calls during appropriate business hours
- Set realistic retry attempts for unanswered calls
- Monitor and adjust based on performance data
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Important Considerations**
- Ensure compliance with local calling regulations
- Maintain up-to-date contact lists
- Respect do-not-call preferences
- Test thoroughly before scaling
</Warning>
